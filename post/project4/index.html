Project 4: Automatically Create Personal Calendar Event (Simon & Schuster)

Overview

  • Efficiency tool developed to reduce redundant work.
  • Automatically creates all-day events based off of group title management sheet.
  • Color coded according to department standards.

Documentation

Link to personal calendar

  • To get started, send me your personal S&S Google Calendar ID.
  • Click on the three dots next to your S&S Calendar, select “Settings and sharing.”

  • Scroll down until you see the “Integrate calendar” heading. Directly under it will be your calendar ID.

  • If you have not sent me your personal calendar ID, you will see this message when they attempt to create a calendar event. If you have sent me their personal calendar ID, check to make sure the DESKTOP field contains your initials (only) with no spaces after.

Check status

  • If the STATUS field is not set to DUE, you will receive this message.

Verify and complete

  • If everything goes well an alert will appear, giving you a chance to double-check that you’ve selected the correct title and inputted the proper due date.
